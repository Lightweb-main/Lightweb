Harmonic Mean

Introduction

Harmonic Mean is a type of average in statistics that measures the average of ratios, & rates such as speed. The Harmonic Mean formula is very similar to the arithmetic mean formula… n 1/x1 * 1/x2 1/x3... Or... n Σ 1/x The Harmonic Mean is the reciprocal of the Arithmetic mean.

Example

An example of a math problem relating to an harmonic mean is…

What is the Harmonic Mean?

1, 4, 7, 9, 12

First we can see that there are five numbers in the data… 5

Now the sum of all the reciprocals of the numbers in the data is the denominator of the answer… n 1 + 1/4 + 1/7 + 1/9 + 1/12

Now we would have to find the Least Common Multiple of all the numbers, 4, 7, 9, and 12… 1.25 + 108/756 + 84/756 + 63/756

1.25 + 255/756

Now we put this under 5… 5 1.25 + 255/756

This fraction equals 3.15… 5 1.25 + 255/756

= 3.15

The Harmonic Mean for this set of numbers is 3.15.
