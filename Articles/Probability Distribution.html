Probability Distribution

Introduction

Probability Distribution in statistics is a description for a variable, basically teliing how much outcomes are favorable in the variable. For example, take a dice for example, a dice usually has 6 sides, every sides probability is 1/6 since there's only one iteration of it and there are 6 of them in total.
