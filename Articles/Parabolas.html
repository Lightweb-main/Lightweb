Parabolas

Introduction

A parabola is a shape commonly found within graphing quadratics and is shaped as a U-shaped curve... A parabolas direction and shape can change vastly depending on the slope of the quadratic that forms it...

General Parabola Formula

The general formula for a parabola can change depending on which direction it faces. For example when a parabola either points up, its general formula would be...

y2 = 4ax

Or when it points down, it's formula would instead be...

y2 = -4ax

And when a parabola points to the right or to the left, it's general formula will now be...

To the right:

x2 = 4ay

To the left:

x2 = -4ay

Summary

Points up: y2 = 4ax

Points down: y2 = -4ax

Points to the left: x2 = 4ay

Points to the right: x2 = -4ay
