Angular Velocity with Linear Velocity

Introduction

Angular Velocity and Linear Velocity are both changes in motion in an object. Linear velocity is the amount of displacement over time, this can be confused with speed which is distance over time. Angular velocity however is angle displacement, the circular motion around a fixed axis, over time.
