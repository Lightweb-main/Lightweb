Introduction

A midpoint in a line segment is the halfway point in it… The formula for finding midpoints is… M = ( x1 + x2 2 , y1 + y2 2 ) An example of finding a midpoint would be two points on a coordinate graph...
M = ( -3 + 4 2 , 2 + 4 2 ) = ( 1 2 ,  6 2 ) = ( 0.5 ,  3 ) So in this example, the midpoint is at, (0.5, 3)... If the math problem is giving you the coordinates for the two points of the line segment like these ones…
