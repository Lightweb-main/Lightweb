Correlation Coefficients

Introduction

Correlation Coefficients are the numbers that range from -1 to 1 that quantifies the strength and direction (linear relationship) between a variable and another with 1 being a perfect correlation and zero being no correlation at all. Correlation Coefficients are often disrupted by outliers (numb ers significantly out of range or an unusual number).
