Arc Length

Introduction

An Arc in mathematics is a portion of a complete circle, not a circle, but a part of it. All arcs have length, known as Arc Length and more arc length means that the arc covers more of the circumference of the circle.

Calculating Arc Length

To calculate an arc's length, you must know the central angle which is the angle at the center of the circle that the arc encompasses. You must also know the radius of the circle which is the length of the line that stretches to the halfway point of the circle. The formula to find arc length is...

While the angle is expressed in Radians

Arc Length = r * θ

While the angle is expressed in degrees

θ 360 * 2πr

Or

θ * π 360 * r
