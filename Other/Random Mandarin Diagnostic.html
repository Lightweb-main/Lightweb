Welcome to the Lightweb Mandarin Diagnostic!

This diagnostic, like others start out easy, then progressively gets more difficult as you answer questions.

Alternatively, if you get questions wrong, the difficulty of the questions will decrease.

The diagnostic will be split into 5 different categories...

  • Introductions
  • Understanding of Radicals
  • Numerical Vocabulary
  • Time and Spacial Vocabulary
  • Food Vocabulary
  • Family Vocabulary
If you do exceptionally well on one of these categories, you will recieve a new 'sub category' with harder questions.

The question topic and number will be displayed in the top left corner with the question itself appearing in the middle.

The choices will be presented in a 2 x 2 grid with four possible answers. Click over one of the choices to select it, then when you're sure about your answer, click the submit button at the bottom right corner.

The questions themselves may be the following:

  • Fill in the blanks
  • Answer the following question.
  • Identify the definition.
  • True or False.

Results will appear at the end of the diagnostic (around 70 - 80 questions)

Pinyin will be included for more basic topics, but more advanced questions will not have pinyin alongside chinese characters. Questions will always be in English.

Click the "Proceed" button to start the diagnostic
