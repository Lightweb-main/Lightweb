Introduction

A cone is a three dimensional shape defined with a flat circle at the bottom and almost like a triangular tip at the top like this...

On a graph however, a cone may have two seperate cones called, nappes...

When a plane (usually a flat square/rectangle), intersects with one of these nappes. It forms a conic section. When a plane intersects a cone, it can generate four shapes, a circle, an ellipse, a parabola, or a hyperbola...
