Superpowers and Abilities Ranked

Welcome to every superpower and supernatural ability ranked by me. These powers are ranked based on every day life so many variables will play a role in ranking.

Every skill will be split into three categories, Main Skills which are skills that define ones character, Subskills or skills that are used alongside the main skill in characters, and passive skills which are techniques, resistances or characteristics that are used regularly.

Ranking Letters

S+ --> S --> S- (Exceeding)

A+ --> A --> A- (Perfect)

B+ --> B--> B- (Great)

C+ --> C --> C- (Satisfactory)

D+ --> D --> D- (Neutral/Lacking)

E+ --> E--> E- (Kind of Useless)

F+ --> F --> F- (Very Bad/Useless)

Main Skills (skills that define a character)

Cryokinesis - 7/15/25

Type: Psychic/Elemental

Description:

Cryokinesis is the power to create and manipulate the force of ice. Usually known as the direct opposite as pyrokinesis.

Independant Variables:

Limiters - Stamina/mana can play a role in this ability

Occupation - Being someone other than superhero/vilain Pyrokinesis would vary in usefulness.

Environment - Cryokinesis can be very or not really effective in certain environments like the desert

Ranking:

Usability --> B

Combat Usage --> S

Destruction --> S+

Utility --> S-

Overall --> S-

Sub-Skills(skills that are numerous among a single character)

Flying - 7/16/25

Type: Movement

Description:

Flying is the act of levitating above the ground and moving (usually at fast speeds) in a certain direction. Flying can be done instinctively and on purpose. Flying can be seen as one of the most primitive skills in superhero media.

Independant Variables:

Speed - The speed of flying can greatly impact the usefulness of flying.

Occupation - If you work far away from your home, then flying would be 100% a good skill.

Limitations - In some media, flying takes concentration and stamina to correctly perform.

Ranking:

Usability --> B+

Combat Usage --> A-

Destruction --> E

Utility --> A+

Overall --> C+ or B-

Teleportation - 7/16/25

Type: Movement

Description:

Teleportation is the ability to travel to a place (usually far away) very quickly. So quickly in fact that it looks instant. Many characters move at the speed of light or FTL (faster than light) when teleporting.

Independant Variables:

Occupation - If you work far away from your home, then teleportation would be a life saver.

Ranking:

Usability --> A+

Combat Usage --> A

Destruction --> E

Utility --> S

Overall --> A

Passive Skills (skills that characters have on default or use passively)

Healing Factor - 7/16/25

Type: Healing

Description:

A healing factor is the ability to regenerate wounds and injuries much faster than the average human. Most extraterrestial characters already do have a healing factor.

Independant Variables:

Conditions - Some characters that have healing factor would need a certain substance, environment, feeling, etc.

Combat Frequency - The more you fight/train, the more helpful a healing factor is

Speed - The speed of regeneration could play a massive role in usefulness

Ranking:

Usability --> A+

Combat Usage --> S

Destruction --> F-

Utility --> B+

Overall --> B+ or A-
